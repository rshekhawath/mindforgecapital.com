The strategy

What it does

The MultiFactor MultiAsset strategy allocates capital across six distinct asset classes that span Indian equities (large-cap, next-50, midcap), international equities (NASDAQ), fixed income (Bharat Bond) and gold. This is not a fixed allocation — it is a dynamic, signal-driven system that shifts weight every month based on which assets are showing the strongest risk-adjusted momentum.

At its heart, the strategy asks a simple question each week: given current price trends, volatility, and momentum, which assets deserve more capital and which deserve less? The answer is computed mathematically — no human discretion enters the process.

The result is a portfolio that naturally tilts toward assets in uptrends while reducing exposure to assets in downtrends. When equity markets are turbulent, the model often moves weight toward gold or fixed income. When Indian markets are running, equity ETFs attract larger allocations.

This strategy is designed to be the most accessible entry point into systematic investing — with a recommended investment of ₹1,00,000 and a monthly subscription of ₹199.

Six assets, one model. Each month, the strategy allocates across Nifty 50 ETF, Nifty Next 50 ETF, Nifty Midcap 150 ETF, Gold ETF, Bharat Bond ETF and NASDAQ ETF — all available on Indian exchanges as liquid ETFs. You can implement this portfolio with a standard demat account.
Defensive by design. The model penalises assets with high recent volatility and cuts exposure to assets trading below their long-term trend. This means the portfolio is not purely return-seeking — it is explicitly designed to manage drawdowns, not just chase returns.
Monthly rebalance. Allocations are updated at the start of each calendar month. You receive the new weights and can rebalance your own portfolio in under 15 minutes.

Trend-following core

The strategy rewards assets in sustained uptrends and systematically reduces weight on assets showing deteriorating price momentum across multiple timeframes.

Volatility-adjusted

High-volatility assets receive a score penalty proportional to how much more volatile they are relative to the universe. This naturally shifts weight toward calmer assets in turbulent markets.

Downtrend protection

Any asset trading below its 10-month moving average receives a significant score haircut, systematically reducing allocation to assets in confirmed downtrends.

True diversification

Six assets from genuinely different economic drivers — Indian equity, global tech, gold and sovereign bonds — provide diversification that goes beyond simply holding multiple stocks.

ETF-only universe

All six instruments are highly liquid NSE-listed ETFs. No small-cap illiquidity, no single-stock concentration risk, no complex derivatives. Simple to execute.

Fully transparent

Every month you receive the exact allocation weights. Every rebalance is documented. The model never changes its rules mid-cycle — what you sign up for is what you get.

Who it is for

The right fit

The MultiFactor MultiAsset strategy is designed for investors who want a disciplined, rules-based approach to asset allocation without having to decide each month whether to be in equities, gold or bonds. The model makes that call systematically.

This strategy is particularly appropriate for investors who are new to systematic investing, have a moderate risk tolerance, or want to complement an existing equity portfolio with an uncorrelated, dynamically managed allocation.
