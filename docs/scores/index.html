The Integrity Score ranks each company against the whole NSE universe on its latest available fundamentals. It is a research starting point, not investment advice or a buy/sell recommendation. Ratios for banks & financials can read unusually. Data refreshed daily from public filings.

How the score works

Each stock is ranked against the entire NSE universe on a set of factors. For every factor a stock sits somewhere from the bottom (0) to the top (100) of all companies — those percentile ranks are averaged into lens scores. The Overall Integrity Score is the average of Quality & Value; Growth and Momentum are shown alongside as separate lenses.
