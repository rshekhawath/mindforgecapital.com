Ranking the universe on Quality & Value factors

Scores rank each company against the whole NSE universe on its latest available fundamentals — they are a research starting point, not investment advice. Ratios for banks & financials can read unusually. Data refreshed monthly.

How the scores work

Each stock is ranked against the entire NSE universe on a set of fundamental factors. For every factor a stock sits somewhere from the bottom (0) to the top (100) of all companies — those percentile ranks are averaged into two pillar scores, and the pillars are averaged into the Overall score.
