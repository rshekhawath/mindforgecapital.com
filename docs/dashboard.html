How to invest in 4 steps

1

Set your capital

Enter how much you want to invest at the top. The Allocation and Shares columns update instantly.

2

Open your broker

Zerodha, Groww, ICICI Direct — any broker works. Use the Copy tickers button to paste the list at once.

3

Place the orders

Use the Shares column as your quantity for each ticker. Limit orders near rec. price are fine on liquid names.
